Project Overview

This case study focuses on analyzing historical sales data for 6 months from Bright Coffee Shop to support the newly appointed CEO in making data-driven business decisions aimed at increasing revenue and improving product performance. The objective was to extract meaningful insight to help us understand the perfomance of the shops and know where to improve to better production.

Key Insights

Top-performing products generated the highest revenue contribution
Sales were significantly stronger during morning hours
Afternoon periods showed relatively lower performance
Certain products consistently underperformed
Seasonal analysis indicated stronger sales in winter compared to summer

Tools & Technologies Used

Microsoft Excel
Databricks
Canva (presentation and Gant chart)
Miro (Architecture Planning)
CSV / Excel datasets
Pivot Tables & Charts

Recommendations

Maximize Mornings peak
Fix afternoon slump by add promotions
Seasonal Strategy maybe add iced coffee, smoothies in summer
Product and Menu Optimization by removing slow selling items
Marketing and customer engagement
Operations and staffing efficiency
Collecting data and continuous monitoring

Closing Summary

This case study demonstrates how data analytics can transform raw sales data into actionable business insights. The findings support smarter decisions on product performance, peak sales periods, and revenue growth strategies, showcasing practical skills in SQL, data visualization, and business reporting.
